What's in it for me?

Is it legit?

OnlyFarms was coded from scratch by real full-stack developers-in-training, who mostly know how to use bcrypt. You can trust OnlyFarms with your farm data. We think.

Are there cookies?

You bet. But they're for functional purposes only. OnlyFarms is not tracking you at this time, and we'll be sure to let you know if that changes. By using OnlyFarms you agree to allow functional cookies.
